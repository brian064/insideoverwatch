what is inside overwatch?

Overwatch is a 6-on-6, team-based shooter by Blizzard. It features a wide array of heroes to choose from, each with their own unique abilitites and backstories.

Inside Overwatch aims to provide you a stream-lined source of information on each of these unique characters from the game.
